# Funded Trader Program - Payout Requests

This document outlines how funded traders can request payouts, the eligibility requirements, and the processing timeline.

---

## Payout Schedule

- Payouts may be requested **once every 14 days**.
- Approved payouts are processed within **7 days** of submission.

Please ensure you are within your 14-day cycle before submitting a request.
