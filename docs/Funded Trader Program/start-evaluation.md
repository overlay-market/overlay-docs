# Start Evaluation

The evaluation phase is the first step of the Funded Trader Program.

To qualify for a funded account, traders must complete a performance challenge based on:

- Required trading volume  
- Required net PnL  

There are no drawdown limits during evaluation.

## How Evaluation Works

During evaluation:

- You trade using your locked collateral.
- You must reach both:
  - The required trading volume
  - The required net PnL target

Both conditions must be satisfied to qualify.

There are no daily loss limits or maximum drawdown rules.
