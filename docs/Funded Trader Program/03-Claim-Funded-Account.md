## Before Funding Is Granted

Once your evaluation is verified:

- Your locked collateral is refunded.
- Your evaluation profits are returned.

After the refund is processed, your funded account will be activated.

---

## Review Process

The team verifies:

- Required trading volume achieved  
- Required net PnL achieved  
- No abuse or manipulation  

If approved:

- Refund is processed.
- Funded capital allocation is granted.
- Funded trader status is assigned.

---

## Funded Account Structure

- 80% of profits go to the trader.
- 20% of profits go to the foundation.
- Payouts occur every 14 days.
